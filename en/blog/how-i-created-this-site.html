The Name

So what is Hammerill? Well, let's just say it's a direct link to me.

Imagine me being 11 years old at some English class:

"H-A-M-M-E-R" - "Hammer". It is a hand tool, kids.

...

Kirill! What does "hammer" mean?

And this was the moment when I thought to merge the two words: "hammer" and my own name "Kirill" into one: Hammerill. I didn't really care about the meaning or anything, it just sounded cool to me.

Since then I've been putting it as my nickname everywhere.

And I came up with a logo, literally a hammer with diamond corners.

The old restored logo of Hammerill made by me in Paint in 2016.

Interestingly, only these diamond corners are still there. You can see them as two blue squares in light mode or two white squares in dark mode as the logo of this site.

I will talk about history of this nickname at the main page when I finish the parallax effect. For now, let's go on.
